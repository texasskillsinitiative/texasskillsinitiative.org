Accessibility Statement

Effective date: February 12, 2026
Last updated: February 12, 2026

Texas Skills Initiative is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability.

Accessibility Goals

  • Design with clear structure and readable typography.
  • Provide keyboard-accessible navigation where possible.
  • Use color contrast that supports readability.

Feedback

If you encounter accessibility issues, please contact: data@texasskillsinitiative.org. We will work to address issues in a timely manner.
