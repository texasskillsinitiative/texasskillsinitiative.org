Security Statement

Effective date: September 15, 2025
Last updated: February 20, 2026

Texas Skills Initiative applies reasonable safeguards to protect information submitted through this site. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

What We Do

  • Use HTTPS to protect data in transit.
  • Restrict access to form processing and data storage.
  • Limit the data we collect to what is needed for regional engagement.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not request or store passwords through this site.
  • We do not use third-party login providers.

Responsible Disclosure

If you believe you have found a security issue, please report it to: data@texasskillsinitiative.org.
